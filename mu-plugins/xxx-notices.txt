function slickstack_admin_notices() { ?>

<div class="notice notice-warning">
<p><strong>June 29, 2019:</strong> We've temporarily disallowed the Tracking Code Manager plugin by IntellyWP because it was causing fatal errors browser redirect loops... the plugin appears semi-abandoned, but we are hoping to whitelist it again if the author can release some patches to the fatal errors it was causing. A reliable alternative for loading many different tracking codes is Google Tag Manager, but always try to load GTM in the footer, not header, for better performance. You can also choose to load tracking scripts for non-Admin users or logged-out users only, to avoid tracking your staff or customers. Using GTM may also help your company more easily abide by legal compliance rules such as the GDPR in Europe, or otherwise. The important thing is to remove scripts from your GTM settings when you no longer need them, otherwise it adds lots of "bloat" to your website and can hurt your frontend performance and loading speeds.</p>
